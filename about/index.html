About

Our Goal

The primary goal of this project was to develop mobile applications that used the Tendril Connect API.

We quickly realized that working with public APIs in Android is kind of a pain. We pivoted to focus on developing a library that would help Developers interface with the Tendril Connect API. The result of this project is a working Java/Android Library for interfacing with the Tendril API, and two accompanying demo Android Applications that use the Cirrus Library.

Senior Project @ CU

This project was part of the 2012 Computer Science Senior Projects at the University of Colorado. Senior Project is a two semester sequence in which teams of students complete a substantial "real world" project provided by sponsors drawn from both industry and research organizations. These projects are developed under the direction of the course instructor and members of the sponsoring organization. Since its inception in the 1987-1988 academic year, 1446 students have completed 343 projects as part of Senior Project.

About Tendril

Tendril is the Energy Platform company, delivering end-to-end consumer engagement products, applications and services powered by Tendril Connect™—an open, secure and scalable platform that takes the complexity out of the Energy Internet and creates a dialogue between energy service providers and their customers. With easy to integrate solutions such as Demand Response and Energy Efficiency, Tendril offers unparalleled insight into energy decisions allowing providers to balance demand, mitigate peaks in real time and minimize operational costs. Consumers are consistently engaged using proven behavioral science models, allowing energy service providers to deploy energy programs while giving their customers the ability to manage their energy usage and costs. Tendril gives energy service providers and their customers the power to solve today’s energy challenges together.
